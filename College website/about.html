Our Story

A Journey of 37 Years

Olympus University was founded in 1987 by Dr. Arjun Mehta, an IIT Bombay alumnus and Padma Bhushan awardee, with a single vision: to create a world-class institution on Indian soil that could compete with the best universities globally.

Starting with just 3 departments and 120 students, Olympus today spans 200 acres with 12 schools, 15,000+ students, and research collaborations with MIT, IIT Delhi, and ISRO. Our alumni lead organizations in 60+ countries, from Silicon Valley startups to the Indian Administrative Service.
