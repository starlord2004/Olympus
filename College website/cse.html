About the Department

Excellence in Computer Science

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering at Olympus University is one of the finest in South India, consistently ranked among the top 10 by NIRF. With state-of-the-art labs in AI, Cybersecurity, Cloud Computing, and Blockchain, we offer students an environment to not just learn — but build, innovate, and launch.

Our alumni work at Google, Microsoft, Amazon, Meta, and leading Indian startups. Established in 1987 as the founding department, CSE continues to set benchmarks in teaching, research, and industry collaboration.
