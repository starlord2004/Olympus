How to Apply

Admission Process

1

Online Application

Fill out the detailed application form and submit relevant academic documents.

2

Entrance Exam

Appear for O-CET (Olympus Common Entrance Test) or submit national scores (JEE/CAT).

3

Interview

Shortlisted candidates will undergo a personal interview via video conference.

Enrollment

Receive provisional admission offer and complete fee payment.
