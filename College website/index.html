About Us

More Than a University. A Legacy.

Founded in 1987 by visionary educator Dr. Arjun Mehta, Olympus University has grown from a single engineering college to a full-fledged deemed university with 12 departments, 47 programs, and a sprawling 200-acre campus in the heart of Bengaluru. We have produced CEOs, scientists, artists, and changemakers who lead organizations across 60 countries.
